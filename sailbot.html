Sailbot

I am currently the software and electrical team lead for SailBot
The club programs, designs, and fabricates an autonomous sailboat to compete in a national competition.

As the software and electrical team lead I am responsible for leading a group of 10ish people in creating all the software required for the boat to sail, navigate around and between buoys, and avoid collisions with other boats autonomously. My team is also responsible for sourcing and managing the sensors and motors required for the boat to sail, as well as establishing a method of wirelessly communicating with the boat from the shore which is often more than 100m away.
